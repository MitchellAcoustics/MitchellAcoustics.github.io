TU Delft Pi-Lab Talk: Soundscape Designs

lecture
Invited Lecture delivered at the IDE Co-Lab at TU Delft
Author
Affiliation

Andrew Mitchell

IEDE @ University College London

Published

May 11, 2025

Abstract

This talk will cover the latest work on soundscape monitoring, assessment, and prediction. this will focus on soundscape circumplex analysis methods, a framework for practical soundscape engineering and interventions. In the talk, Dr. Mitchell will discuss the application of soundscape principles for the design and monitoring of hospital environments and the creation of open-source software for soundscape analysis (Soundscapy)
